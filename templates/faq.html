Compilation

We are going to do the compilation. Although this may sound tedious, the concept of Unearthworks is recommending quality fanfictions to the online community. The problem being, there exist many low quality stories out online, such as those with poor grammar and spelling as well as storyline. What Unearthworks does is pick out those stories that are of better quality based on our Sample Rubric and categorise them to allow users to find them easily. Due to the limitation that we are filtering out the higher quality fanfictions, there is a need for manual compilation instead of automated ones.

Difference between Unearthworks and other websites

Till date, there are no existing websites that recommend quality fanfictions and arrange them according to the different genres. There are only a couple of blog posts about the personal preference/favourites of the blog owners. As an avid reader of fanfictions, we find it a hassle to have to browse through a large number of fanfics in order to search for good quality ones, hence the need for websites such as Unearthworks.
